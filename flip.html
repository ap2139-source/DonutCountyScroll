Meet the Cast

Donut County’s full of strange but familiar faces. Everyone’s got their own way of dealing with the chaos
Some laugh it off, some complain, some just go with it. Get to know the people and creatures that make this place what it is.

BK
A trash-loving raccoon who caused all the chaos (but swears it wasn’t his fault).
Mira
BK’s best friend and the voice of reason. She’s trying to fix what he broke.
Chef
A yellow cat who runs Cat Soup with his bird sidekick. Don’t mention the cockroaches.
Coco
A friendly alligator and coworker. Always chill, even when the town’s falling apart.
Coyote
The wise old owner who sold his Donut Shop to the raccoons. Big mistake.
Kevin
Mira’s dog and professional bread thief. Never underestimate him around carbs.
Morsel
A little green guy with a cast and bad luck, but he’s still smiling.
Possum
A conspiracy-loving hacker who actually turns out to be right about everything.
Potter
A calm, creative mom who makes pottery and tries to keep things together.
Pup
Potter’s curious kid who can’t resist poking at trouble. Literally.
Trash King
The ruler of raccoons and garbage — obsessed with control and shiny things.
